# Fort Worth Permit Estimator - Deployment Guide

## Quick Start

Your website is ready to deploy! Follow these simple steps:

### Step 1: Create a GitHub Account (Free)
1. Go to https://github.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email
5. Done!

### Step 2: Upload Your Website to GitHub
1. Go to https://github.com/new
2. Name it: `fort-worth-permit-estimator`
3. Click "Create repository"
4. Follow the instructions to upload files

**OR use this easier method:**
1. Go to https://github.com/upload
2. Drag and drop the `fort_worth_permit_estimator` folder
3. Commit the files

### Step 3: Deploy to Vercel (Free)
1. Go to https://vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Click "Continue with GitHub"
4. Authorize Vercel
5. Click "New Project"
6. Select your `fort-worth-permit-estimator` repository
7. Click "Deploy"
8. **Wait 2-3 minutes**
9. Your website is LIVE! 🎉

### Step 4: Get Your Live URL
After deployment, Vercel will give you a URL like:
`https://fort-worth-permit-estimator.vercel.app`

**Share this link with people!**

### Step 5: Add Your Domain (Optional)
If you bought a domain name:
1. In Vercel, go to your project
2. Click "Settings"
3. Click "Domains"
4. Add your domain name
5. Follow the instructions to connect it

### Step 6: Add Google AdSense (Make Money!)
1. Go to https://google.com/adsense
2. Sign up with your Google account
3. Add your website URL
4. Wait for approval (3-7 days)
5. Get your Publisher ID
6. Update these files with your Publisher ID:
   - `index.html` (find `ca-pub-xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx`)
   - `src/components/AdSense.tsx` (find `data-ad-client`)
7. Commit and push changes
8. Vercel will automatically redeploy
9. Ads will appear on your site!
